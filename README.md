#  Moushaf Mohammadi
Dataset of the Noble Qur'an in the reading of Warsh according to Nafie by the way of Al-Azraq, which is the most widely used version in Morocco & Andalusia.

This is scraped from [the app](https://www.habous.gov.ma/fr/annonce-affaires-islamiques/6552-almoshaf-almohammadi-mis-en-ligne.html) made by the Moroccan Ministry of Religious Endowements and the Mohammed VI Foundation for the Edition of the Noble Quran 

The Noble Qur'an, in its quality as an endowment (waqf), does not have any copyright restrictions upon it.
